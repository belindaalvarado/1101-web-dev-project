Welcome to Lorcana

Lorcana is a collectible card game released by Ravensburger in collaboration with The Walt Disney Company. The game features characters from Disney movies, both in original and reimagined art styles, set in a magical world called Lorcana.Players use "magic inks" to assemble teams of Disney characters and compete in various challenges
